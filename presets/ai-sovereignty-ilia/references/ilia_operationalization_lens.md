# ILIA Operationalization Lens

Use this only after source-level synthesis is complete.

For each cross-source finding, ask:

1. What dimension of AI sovereignty does this imply?
2. What is the unit of analysis: country, state institution, research ecosystem, firm ecosystem, data domain, infrastructure layer, or society?
3. What observable proxy could ILIA plausibly measure?
4. Does the proxy measure capacity, agency, power, autonomy, resilience, legitimacy, or dependency?
5. What data source could support it?
6. Is the variable comparable across Latin American countries?
7. What would the variable fail to capture?
8. Could the indicator reward authoritarian control, extractive infrastructure, or symbolic policy without real agency?
9. Is this already covered by ILIA 2025/2026, adjacent to an existing subindicator, or a genuinely new construct?

Do not force the literature into ILIA too early. First synthesize faithfully; then operationalize.
